2021

- New member -

Apr 1, 2021.
Florencia Belen Chocho has joined our group with a scholarship from the National Agency for the Promotion of Research, Technology, and Innovation (ANPCyT, Argentina) to pursue her Ph.D. degree. Her project is related to the characterization of exosomes subpopulations from mesenchymal stromal cells and their potential use for cardiovascular therapies.. Congratulations and welcome aboard!

- New member -

Mar 1, 2021.
Juan Ignacio Pardo has joined our group to pursue his Ph.D. degree. His project is related to the use of bone marrow-derived mesenchymal stromal cells for the regeneration of lesions derived from diabetic foot disease. Welcome aboard!

2020

- International meeting -

May 28-29, 2020.
Our group presented the following three abstracts at the International Society for Cell and Gene Therapy (ISCT) Annual Meeting. Paris, France.
Oral presentation: "Bioassay standardization to assess exosomes anti-inflammatory activity in vitro". Malvicini R, Santa-Cruz D, Sanmartin C, Pacienza N, Yannarelli G.
Poster presentation: "Anti-inflammatory exosomes mediate the beneficial effect of mesenchymal stem cell therapy on lung preservation". Pacienza N, Santa-Cruz D, Malvicini R, Sanmartin C, Marcos M, Robledo O, Bertolotti A, Yannarelli G.
Poster presentation: "Mesenchymal stem cell therapy maximizes lung preservation by preventing ischemic organ injury". Santa Cruz D, Malvicini R, Sanmartin C, Marcos M, Robledo O, Bertolotti A, Yannarelli G, Pacienza N.

- New member -

Jan 2, 2020.
María Cecilia Sanmartin has joined our group with a scholarship from the National Agency for the Promotion of Research, Technology, and Innovation (ANPCyT, Argentina) to pursue her Ph.D. degree. Her project is related to the role that extracellular vesicles play in the development of the bone marrow pre-metastatic niche in patients with advanced breast cancer. Congratulations and welcome aboard!
